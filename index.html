About My Routine

My Routine is an interactive fitness website designed to help you build structure, stay motivated, and track your personal fitness goals. The platform supports both beginners and more experienced users in creating and maintaining an effective workout routine.

Features

The site excists out of 3 main sections: Training, Planner, and Progress. On the training page you will be able to put together a detailed schematic of the different exercises you want inside of your routine. On the Planner page you can schedule your workouts and track your discipline. And on the Progress page you can monitor your improvements and new achievements.

Goal of the website

The goal of My Routine is to make fitness more accessible, organized, and motivating. By combining planning tools, visual guidance, and progress tracking, the website helps you stay committed and achieve your fitness goals in a structured and efficient way.
